Our Process

How We Guide You to the Right Career

A structured, personalised process designed to give every student clarity and confidence.

01

Free Discovery Session

We begin with a no-cost introductory call to understand your background, challenges, and goals. No commitment required.

02

Psychometric Assessment

You complete a scientifically validated aptitude and personality assessment — online or in-person — in under 90 minutes.

03

In-Depth Report Analysis

Our counsellor analyses your results and prepares a personalised career report mapping your strengths to viable career options.

04

Counselling Session

A one-on-one or family counselling session where we walk through the report, answer every question, and build an actionable roadmap.

05

Action Plan & Follow-Up

You receive a detailed written action plan. Our team continues to support you with follow-up sessions as you progress.

Career counselling is a professional guidance service that helps individuals identify their strengths, interests, values, and personality traits to make informed decisions about their education and career. It's most beneficial for students after Class 10, after Class 12, during college, or anyone feeling uncertain about their professional direction.

Ideally, students should begin career exploration around Class 8-9 so they can make an informed stream choice in Class 10. However, it's never too late — we work with college students and working professionals as well. Early guidance prevents costly mistakes like choosing the wrong stream or degree.

A psychometric assessment is a scientifically designed test that evaluates your aptitude (logical, verbal, numerical, spatial reasoning), personality type, interests, and emotional intelligence. The test takes approximately 60-90 minutes, and our counsellors then interpret the results in detail during a one-on-one session to identify best-fit career options.
